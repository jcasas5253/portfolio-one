ABOUT ME

Overview

Hello, I'm Jacob Casas, a passionate Full Stack Software Engineer based in the sunny landscapes of Southern California. My enthusiasm for technology and programming is matched only by my love for adventure. When I'm not reading lines of code, you can often find me embracing the outdoors. I love to snowboard, hike, and scuba dive, among many other activities. My journey into the world of software engineering began with a Full Stack Development course offerd from the University of California, Irvine (UCI). I earned a certificate in the course, where I honed my skills and gained a solid foundation in web development. Currently, I'm continuing my educational journey at Southern New Hampshire University (online), pursuing a degree in Computer Science. This academic pursuit keeps me wanting to stay at the forefront of technology, enabling me to tackle complex challenges and contribute to innovative solutions.

Responsive

My programs and websites are always built with a mobile first mindset. This means that when I design and build programs or websites, I prioritize the mobile user experience above all else. Why? Because mobile devices have become the primary means of accessing the internet and digital content for a vast majority of users.

Full Stack

I possess the expertise and experience to create a robust web presence starting from scratch. This encompasses both front-end and back-end development, ensuring that every aspect of a website or web application is meticulously designed, developed, and optimized.

Problem Solver

I approach every issue with a structured and analytical mindset, enabling me to efficiently identify the root cause and devise optimal solutions.

Passion

Programming and web development are not just a profession for me; they are my passion. This dedication drives me to excel in my career, engage with the developer community, and pursue personal projects with endless enthusiasm.

PROJECTS

Movie Finder

This application allows the user to search for any movie or television show of their chosing. The application will then provide the user with information and links on where they can watch their desired movie or television program. The user can also navigate to the "Movies In Theaters" and "Trending TV" pages.

OPA Visions

This website was built for OPA Visions (Only Publishing Art). It includes their music, photos, music videos, artist section, and shop (created by owner of OPA). This site was built with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Jquery, and Shopify was used for the online store (created by owner of OPA).

Platform Game (Under Development)

This is a 2D platform game that will be available on mobile or PC. You will have one life to reach the top. On your way up, you might miss a platform and fall a long way, or even die from a trap and have to restart. The game is currently under development using the Unity Engine.
